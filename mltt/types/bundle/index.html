Bundle package contains basic information about fibers (needed for weak-equivalence) and fiber bundles, constructions from algebraic topology.

fiber

The fiber of the map $p: E \rightarrow B$ in a point $y: B$ is all points $x: E$ such that $p(x)=y$.

fiber (E B: U) (p: E -> B) (y: B): U = (x: E) * Path B y (p x)

fiber bundle

The fiber bundle $ F \rightarrow E \mapright{p} B$ on a topological space E with fiber F and base B is a structure $(F,E,B,p)$ where F is a layer, E is a total space, B is a base of fibration, and $p: E \rightarrow B$ is a surjective map with following property: for any point $y: B$ exists a neighborhood $U_b$ for which a homeomorphism $f: p^{-1}(U_b) \rightarrow U_b \times F$ making the following diagram commute.
