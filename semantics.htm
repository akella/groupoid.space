Universes

\begin{equation} \tag{Sorts} \dfrac {n : Nat} {*_n} \end{equation}
$$ \begin{equation} \tag{Axioms} \dfrac {i: Nat} {*_i : *_{i+1}} \end{equation} $$
$$ \begin{equation} \tag{Rules} \dfrac {i : Nat, j : Nat} {*_i \rightarrow *_{j} : *_{max(i,j)}} \end{equation} $$



Typing Rules

\begin{equation} \tag{$\Pi$-formation} \dfrac {x:A \vdash B : Type} {\Pi\ (x:A) \rightarrow B : Type} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} \tag{$\lambda$-intro} \dfrac {x:A \vdash b : B} {(\lambda\ (x:A)\ b) : (\Pi\ (x: A)\ B )} \end{equation}
$$ \begin{equation} \tag{App-elimination} \dfrac {f: (\Pi\ (x:A)\ B)\ \ \ a: A} {f\ a : B\ [a/x]} \end{equation} $$
$$ \begin{equation} \tag{$\beta$-computation} \dfrac {x:A \vdash b: B\ \ \ a:A} {(\lambda\ (x:A)\ b)\ a : b[a/x] : B [a/x]} \end{equation} $$
